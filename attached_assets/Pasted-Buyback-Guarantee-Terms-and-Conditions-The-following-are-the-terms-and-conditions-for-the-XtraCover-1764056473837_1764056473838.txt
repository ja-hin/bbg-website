4.1 A claim is valid only if:

4.1.4 The IMEI/Serial Number of device matches the data provided at time of

claimant.
4.2 While exercising Buyback Guarantee, a field executive shall visit the location of
the customer for the pick-up and shall conduct the following checks on the original
device. The customer must make sure that:
4.2.1 The Serial Number (for laptops) / IMEI (for mobiles) of the original device
provided by the customer at the time of purchase of the new device is correct and
there is no mismatch.
4.2.2 The device's screen and the body of the Original Device are not in damaged or
broken condition and the Original Device is in working condition.
4.2.3 There are no cracks in the body of the device or on the screen.
4.2.4 The screen must be intact, and buttons should not be missing or damaged.
4.2.5 The display should not have any spots or dead pixels.
4.2.6 The original box, charger, and accessories are in good working condition. (For
mobiles, headphones are also included).
4.2.7 Screen lock is not active (all forms of screen locks like pattern, pin, password,
fingerprint, face recognition, smart Lock, etc. need to be deactivated).
